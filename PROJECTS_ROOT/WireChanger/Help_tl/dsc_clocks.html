Active Clocks

You can decorate your desktop with beautiful live clocks, most of them are prototypes of real, world-famous clocks. In order to use clocks, Active Desktop and Macromedia Flash plug-in must be available. You can download more clocks in different ways:

Way #1 - clocks can be taken from http://www.colclocks.com. To install them download zip file, find WireChanger installation directory ("C:\Program Files\WiredPlane\WireChanger\" is the most common path) and unpack archive into "Clocks" subdirectory. Restart WireChanger and choose your new clock from tray menu! :^)
