Active Clocks

You can decorate your desktop with beautiful live clocks - most of them are prototypes of real, world-famous clocks. In order to use clocks, Active Desktop must be activated and the Macromedia Flash plug-in must be available. In addition to the clocks included in WireChanger, clocks can be taken from http://www.colclocks.com or from http://www.adamdorman.com. To install them, download the clock`s zip file, install them and then use WireChanger`s "Import flash clock" feature, pointing it into clock`s installation directory. Even more clocks can be found on our clocks collections page.
